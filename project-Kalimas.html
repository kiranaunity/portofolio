AR Geolocation Experience Along Kalimas River

Client: Surabaya City Government
Role: Unity Developer / AR Technologist
Platform: Mobile AR (Android)
Year: 2024

Wisata Surabaya is an Augmented Reality (AR) mobile application designed to enhance tourism along the historic Kalimas River in Surabaya through location-based storytelling. As users walk alongside the river, the app utilizes geolocation to trigger interactive AR experiences directly tied to specific points of interest.

System Flow : User Launches the App → GPS Location Detection → Geolocation Checkpoint Trigger → AR Content Display and Interaction

Key Features

🦎 Geolocation-based Animal Mascot Animations
🏛️ Historical Augmented Landmarks
📚 Educational and Cultural Focus

Technologies Used

Unity + AR Foundation: For cross-platform AR development
Google ARCore: For Android-based AR functionality
GPS & Geolocation APIs: For real-time location detection
3D Audio/Text/3D Assets: For educational content delivery
