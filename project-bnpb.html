AR Evacuation Education Application

Client: Indonesian National Board for Disaster Management (BNPB)
Role: Unity Developer / AR Technologist
Platform: Mobile AR (Android)
Year: 2024

This mobile AR application was developed to support public education and disaster preparedness initiatives in Indonesia. The app allows users to simulate evacuation scenarios in real-time using augmented reality and geolocation technology. By using GPS, users can identify nearby evacuation points, follow AR-guided routes, and receive educational tips throughout their journey. It was designed for urban disaster contexts such as floods, fires, and earthquakes, making disaster response training more accessible and interactive for all age groups.

Key Features

📍 Location-Based AR Navigation
🗺️ Interactive Routing with Estimated Time
🧭 Real-Time AR Directional Arrows
🧠 Disaster Education Content in AR
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Accessible Design for All Ages

Technologies Used

Unity + AR Foundation
Google ARCore: For Android-based AR functionality
Mapbox SDK for Unity
Firebase (optional: user activity logging, analytics)
